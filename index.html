Our Divine Story

Bringing sacred art and devotion together — crafting authentic murtis that carry the blessings of India's heritage artisans since 1990.

Crafting Divinity Since 1990

Divya Murti Mandir was founded with a sacred mission — to preserve and celebrate India's rich tradition of murti craftsmanship. Every statue we offer is handcrafted by skilled artisans in Jaipur, Mathura, and Varanasi.

Our artisans have inherited their craft through generations, ensuring each murti carries not just artistic beauty, but spiritual energy and devotion. We source only the finest materials — Makrana marble, premium stone, and precious metals.

From small home mandirs to large temple installations, we cater to every devotee's need with authenticity, reverence, and excellence.
